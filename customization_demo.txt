Whole Wheat Waffles
Original:
In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, ground cloves, ginger and Flax Seeds; set aside.
Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
In a separate bowl, beat the eggs.
Stir in the milk, oil vanilla and honey.
Mix fruit in dry mixture.
Pour the milk mixture into the flour/fruit mixture; mix lightly till nicely blended.
Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron.
Cook the waffles until golden and crisp.

Title:
Dried Fruit Salad with Philly* Cream
Original:
Bring water, juice and sugar to the boil in a saucepan.
Simmer for 2-3 minutes until sugar has dissolved.
Add dried fruits and cinnamon stick, simmer a further 5 minutes.
Whisk together the Philly, milk and honey until smooth.
Serve fruits warm with Philly cream and toasted almonds.
Requirement: 
Can I add some chocolate and bananas into it?

Simmer for 2-3 minutes until sugar has dissolved.

Serve fruits warm with Philly cream, a sprinkle of extra chopped dark chocolate on top for added texture, and toasted almonds

Title:
Steak & Asparagus Wraps
Original:
Pour marinade over steak in resealable plastic bag.
Seal bag; turn to evenly coat steak with dressing.
Refrigerate at least 30 min.
to marinate.
Meanwhile, preheat grill to medium heat.
Remove steak from marinade; discard bag and marinade.
Grill steak 13 to 16 min.
or until medium doneness (160F), turning occasionally.
Meanwhile, steam asparagus 8 to 10 min.
or until crisp-tender.
Cut steak into thin strips.
Place 1 to 2 steak strips and about 2 asparagus spears on each tortilla strip; roll up.
Secure with frilled toothpicks or red bell pepper ties, if desired.
Serve warm.
Requirement：
Can you simplifythe steps to make it easy and short?
Customization:
While preheating your grill, quickly marinate the steak with your preferred seasoning or marinade.
Grill the steak to your desired level of doneness. Simultaneously, grill the asparagus spears until they're slightly charred and tender.

Quickly assemble the wraps by placing steak strips and asparagus spears on each tortilla strip. Roll them up.
Serve immediately while still warm.
